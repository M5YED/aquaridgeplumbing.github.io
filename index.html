Did you know...?

- On average, a tap that drips once per second wastes about 5 litres of water a day. That’s a whopping 1,825 litres over a year!
- In cold weather, dripping taps can lead to frozen pipes, which can then burst, causing significant water damage.
- The water wasted by a single dripping tap may seem insignificant, but when multiplied across thousands of homes, it becomes a considerable environmental concern.

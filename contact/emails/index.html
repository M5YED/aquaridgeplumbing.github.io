Intro to AI

Some of our emails contain a disclaimer at the bottom of the footer. The disclaimer talks about AI and how there may be errors in the email due to the "presence of AI". We simply mean that some of our emails are created, modified and sent by AI automations. We use AI to speed up the time it takes to reach our vast amount of customers and viewers, so we can focus more on delivering our services. Our AI-assisted emails may be sent during different times of the day, including the night, however, we try our best to avoid communications at times of rest.

We understand the danger of AI, especially due to its unfamiliar nature for most customers. We strive to make the AI software we use as safe and secure as possible to prevent any harmful scenarios. We try to rectify or correct any mistakes that we see which may have been sent to customers before we could intercept the email. You may see us issue corrections by referencing the email subject line, the error within the email and the correction to be made.

Our AI provider/partner is Assetflow Automations. We work with them to create software that is beneficial for our company. If you face any errors or inconveniences with our AI software, or with any other software that we use, please report the error to support@aquaridgeplumbing.com.

Staying Safe Using Emails

To stay safe using our emails, we recommend always checkink who the email came from. We will only send you an email with an email address formatted as "{...}@aquaridgeplumbing.com". Any other email address is not ours and we advise you not not click any links or respond to the email in any way. We also ask you to not make any payments to users with emails that are not ours. We sometimes collect payments over emails, so it is important to make sure that you are talking to the correct person before you make any payments. We take no responsibility for any actions that may take place from fraudulent emails. It is up to you, the customer or audience, to keep yourself safe from scams.

Another crucial point to note about emails from us, is that each customer is assigned a 6-digit "sort-code". This code is connected to your data, stored in our databases, and will never change(unless you delete your data and then restore it). If you're unsure about whether an email came from us, we advise you to recheck your sort code from previous emails to make sure that they are the same. If you receive an email that does not have your sort code, or has a sort code which is different from your usual sort code, please report the email to support@aquaridgeplumbing.com immediately! Please do not interact with, or respond to emails that do not contain your sort code/contain the wrong sort code.

We generate random sort codes, so 2 people may have the same sort code. However, our systems integrate other data along with your sort code to grant you access to our software. If someone has the same sort code as you, they will not be able to access, update or delete your data. Your data is protected and will always remain private&confidential.

Policy Update Emails

We sometimes make updates to our general or privacy policies to stay within certain legal laws and regulations. As some of these changes may impact you, as our customers or audience, or your data that we have, stored in our databases, we are required to inform you of changes that were made/when they were made. We present a timestamp to show you when the policies were last modified, so that cases that arise, may be dealt with judging by the policies that were in place during that specific time.

Some policies do not affect all customers or viewers, and is directed towards certain types of individuals or organisations(ie. commercial customers, customers with a full project). We try to clearly highlight a policy if it is directed towards certain types of individuals or organisations. Most policies will apply to all customers.

Please note: The general policy is currently inactive and will be revised soon. Anything mentioned or presented within the general policy will not be adhered to, and is not "legally binding", until the policies have been revised.

We also allow 3rd party members(viewers, but not customers) to receive notifications for when we make updates or changes to our policies. We present a submission box under each policy page, for members to provide their email address. This email address gets transported to our database, ready to integrate with our systems. Members cannot currently access, or remove their email address from our database themselves. To remove your email from our policy update notification list, contact support@aquaridgeplumbing.com.

To receive policy update notifications when we update or amend our policies, submit your email below:

We allow customers to unsubscribe from our policy updates using our AI software. We do this so that customers can unsubscribe at their own pace. We tend to get too busy to unsubscribe customers ourselves, so the delay in manually removing email addresses from our policy notification list can extend to days, weeks, or even months.

To remove your email from our policy notification list, we've made some steps for you to follow. This is the quickest way to unsubscribe from our list and should only take around 2 minutes.
Step 1: Open the chat with "Plumbing Assistant". You will find the chat bubble on the bottom-right of your screen.
Step 2: Click the "I want to talk to support" button.
Step 3: Click the "Answer my query" button.
Step 4: Type "Unsubscribe me from the mailing list" and submit the message.
Step 5: Click the "Policy Updates" button.
Step Extra: If you are a customer or have been allocated a sort code, type out your sort code.
Step 6: Type out your email.
Objective Complete

If you are unable to navigate through these instructions, just send a message to support@aquaridgeplumbing.com and say you don't want to receive anymore Policy Update notifications. Please note that the delay time for people who contact us to remove their email address from our policy notification list can extend to a few days, weeks or even months.

We also allow customer access to our database so that customers can delete their data from our database themselves. You can choose to view, update or delete your data.

To remove your data from our database, we've made some steps for you to follow. This is the quickest way to delete your data and should only take around 5 minutes.
Step 1: Open the chat with "Plumbing Assistant". You will find the chat bubble on the bottom-right of your screen.
Step 2: Click the "I want to talk to support" button.
Step 3: Click the "Answer my query" button.
Step 4: Type "Delete my data from the database" and submit the message.
Step 5: Click the "Remove my data" button.
Step 6: Enter your Full Name.
Step 7: Enter your sort code.
Step 8: Follow the assistant's instructions.
Objective Complete

Note: You will not be able to delete your data from our database if we do not have your number and home area/location stored in our database. If you believe we have other data stored about you, contact support@aquaridgeplumbing.com.
